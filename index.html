Research

My research interests lie in the area of vision and language, and language grounding. I work on vision language navigation and text-to-image synthesis. Recently, I am interested in the general impact of language on the visual domain, e.g. underspecification for VLN or language structures for image synthesis. I am also interested in the application of diffusion models.
